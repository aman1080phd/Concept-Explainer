<h2>
  
  nPr = n!/(n-r)! 
  nPr = n\*(n-1)\*(n-2)....n-(r-1).
  
  n = 3 chairs
  r = 5 persons
  
  nPr = 5!/(5-3)! = 120/2 = 60.
  
  nPr = 5\*4\*5-(3-1) = 5\*4\*3 = 60.
  
  In 60 ways 5 persons can sit on 3 chairs
  
</h2>
